Our Purpose

C02 is the biggest contributor to global warning. it is now or never for us to take actions to reduce our carbon footprint. We want to help sensitise our students and staff about how their daily actions contribute to this issue and how individually they can take steps to reduce their emissions. Each of us matters! Every gram saved by a person adds up when everyone is conscious and doing their best. We hope that this website will help you understand your emissions and motivate you to take these steps for the betterment of all.

There is a list of tasks that can be completed daily and challenges, for those that want to go the extra mile, will be released monthly. You can also participate in groups and compete with each other. A leaderboard will show who is the most green among us and special prizes await them. May the best eco person win!
